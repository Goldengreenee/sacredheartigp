About us

Sacred Heart Church, Igatpuri is a Catholic parish cared for by the Dominican Fathers (Order of Preachers - OP).
Located in the peaceful hills of Igatpuri, our parish is a welcoming place of prayer, worship, and community.

Led by Rev. Fr. Xavier Kandulna, OP, we celebrate the Holy Mass, the sacraments, and foster spiritual growth through the Dominican mission of preaching, teaching, and prayer.
We invite you to experience the love of the Sacred Heart of Jesus and the joy of belonging to a faith-filled community. All are welcome at Sacred Heart Church, Igatpuri.
