A Sacred Appeal for the Restoration of Our Church Roof



Dear Beloved Family,

Warm greetings in the grace and peace of our Lord Jesus Christ.

We come to you today not merely with a request, but with a heartfelt invitation to be part
of something sacred — the preservation of our spiritual home, Sacred Heart Church.

Over the years, this holy place has been a sanctuary of prayer, healing, and community.
It has witnessed our baptisms, our weddings, our tears, and our joys. It is here that we
have encountered the living God in the Eucharist and found strength in times of trial.

Today, our church roof stands in urgent need of care. Time and weather have taken
their toll, and the roof now requires rust-resistant painting to prevent further damage
and leakage. Without this essential work, we risk compromising the very shelter that has
so faithfully sheltered us.

The estimated cost for this restoration is ₹1,80,000. We know this is a significant
amount — but we also know the power of faith and unity. Together, as one body in
Christ, we can rise to this occasion.

“Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not
reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver.”
— 2 Corinthians 9:7

Let us not allow the house of the Lord to fall into disrepair. Let us respond with the same
love and generosity that God has shown us. Every contribution — whether large or small
— is a sacrifice of love, a testimony of faith, and a gift to future generations who will
worship under this very roof.

We humbly ask you to prayerfully consider how you can support this mission. Donations
can be made at the parish office, through designated envelopes available at the church or online.
